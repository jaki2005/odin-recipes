Lasagna

From pasta salad to rib eye roast, these meals are great options for when you want a delicious dinner but don't have the energy to match your appetite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, oregano, basil, and milk in a large skillet. Cook over medium heat until meat is browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes.
